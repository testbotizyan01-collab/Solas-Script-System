About Solas Script

At Solas Script, we believe that every student deserves a safe space to address their mental health and emotional well-being. Our mission is to create an inclusive digital sanctuary where students can find support, resources, and tools to manage stress and improve their overall wellness.

Mental health is not just the absence of illness—it's about thriving. We're dedicated to breaking down stigma, making mental health support accessible, and fostering a community where students feel valued, heard, and supported.

Our platform combines psychology-approved techniques, interactive wellness tools, and emergency support resources. Whether you need to decompress with calming games, journal your thoughts, or connect with professional guidance, Solas Script is here for you.

We take pride in partnering with Our Lady of Fatima University to bring you a comprehensive mental health support system. Behind every feature is our commitment to excellence, compassion, and your well-being.

Founded on the values of the OLFU community—Respect, Integrity, Service, and Excellence—Solas Script stands as a beacon of hope for students navigating the complexities of modern life. We believe that mental wellness is a journey, not a destination, and we're honored to walk alongside you every step of the way.

Your well-being matters. Your voice matters. And you matter. Welcome to Solas Script, where every student finds their sanctuary.

Mental Health & Wellness Support

Mental health refers to our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It includes our ability to handle stress, relate to others, and make decisions. Mental health is important at every stage of life, from childhood through adulthood.

Common signs of anxiety include excessive worry, restlessness, difficulty concentrating, muscle tension, sleep disturbances, rapid heartbeat, and feeling on edge. If these symptoms persist, consider reaching out to a mental health professional.

Effective stress management techniques include regular exercise, meditation, deep breathing exercises, maintaining a healthy sleep schedule, spending time in nature, talking to trusted friends or family, and seeking professional help if needed. Solas Script also offers games and journaling tools to help you unwind.

If you're experiencing depression, it's important to reach out for help. Talk to a trusted friend, family member, or mental health professional. Don't isolate yourself. Resources like Solas Script can provide support, and the OLFU Guidance Services are available 24/7 for students in crisis.
