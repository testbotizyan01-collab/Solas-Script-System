Our Lady of Fatima University (OLFU)

History

Our Lady Fatima University is a prestigious university established in 1996, known for its outstanding medical expertise. It offers numerous high-quality educational services in Quezon City at its buildings in Regalado Avenue, Fatima Eye and Rehabilitation Center Building, and Hilltop Subdivision in Lagro.

This exceptional university distinguishes itself as an inclusive university that nurtures the academic environment of the students, where mental health is one of the great highlights, through the Center for Guidance Services (CGS). Embracing the university’s mission by “improving man as man”

The campus offers a safe and confidential space for each Fatimanian who needs a fulfilling hand for supporting the student’s academic pressure and emotional well-being. The counseling services are designed to offer regular mental health seminars and “Kamustahan” (check-in) sessions with professional counselors.

Core Mission

OLFU works through the holistic formation of individuals who are aspiring, compassionate, honorable, inspiring, ethical, visionary, efficient, and responsible. The university believes in educating not just the minds but the hearts of its students.
