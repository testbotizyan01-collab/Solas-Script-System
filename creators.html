Our Shared Mission

Together, as a dedicated team from Our Lady of Fatima University's HUMSS strand, we created Solas Script to fulfill a simple but powerful vision: ensuring that every student has access to mental health support, professional guidance, and a safe digital space to process their emotions and find peace.

Every feature, every color, every line of code reflects our commitment to student well-being and our belief that mental health is a journey we don't have to take alone.
